The Legacy of Coon Creek and our Vision for Golden State Shooting Center

Embrace the Legacy, Experience the Future: Welcome to Golden State Shooting Center

For decades, Coon Creek Trap and Skeet Club has been a cherished cornerstone of the Northern California shooting community. Renowned for its welcoming atmosphere and well-maintained grounds, it has hosted countless state championships, charity events, and provided a supportive environment for shooters of all levelsâ€‹.

As we turn the page to a new chapter, we are excited to announce our rebranding as Golden State Shooting Center. This transformation is not just a change of name but a renewed commitment to our founding principles. We honor the rich legacy of Coon Creek by preserving the traditions and community spirit that have made it a beloved destination for shooters. Our storied past is the foundation upon which we are building a dynamic future.

Golden State Shooting Center is dedicated to modernizing our facilities to offer an unparalleled shooting experience. Our investments in state-of-the-art equipment and infrastructure ensure that we meet the evolving needs of today's shooting enthusiasts while maintaining the high standards that our community expects. From enhanced trap and skeet fields to the introduction of advanced sporting clay courses, we are elevating every aspect of our center to provide top-tier amenities and services.

Moreover, we are passionate about fostering the next generation of shooters. Our comprehensive training programs and youth initiatives aim to inspire and develop young talent, ensuring that the future of trap, skeet, and sporting clays is vibrant and thriving. With expert instruction and a supportive community, we are nurturing the skills and confidence of new shooters, preparing them to become the champions of tomorrow.

Join us at Golden State Shooting Center, where the legacy of the past meets the innovation of the future. Experience the best of both worlds: the tradition of Coon Creek and the exciting advancements of a premier modern shooting facility. Together, we are setting the stage for a bright and promising future in the world of shooting sports.

Visit us and be part of our journey as we continue to create memories, build champions, and uphold the spirit of excellence that defines Golden State Shooting Center. Welcome to the new era of shooting sports in California.
